    Virtual Renewable Energy Laboratory

    Using Renewable Energy Experiments
    to Enhance Student Engagement in STEM.

    Welcome to the Virtual Renewable Energy Laboratory. This web site is an internet portal in which pre-college and college students can experiment with distributed power generation system, and collect data for their engineering activities.

    Go to Lectures

    The project is funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF) Improving Undergraduate STEM Education program (DUE-1712146) from June 2017 to May 2020.
