    About Us

    We are a team of students working in Technology Department in Northern Illinois University. Our Goal is to enlighten young minds to the work power engineers do. To achieve this goal, we have created a simulation model of a buck converter that can be accessed online without the use of special software that these programs using use that may be very costly to have access to. How will people come to know about engineering when the tools we engineers often use are so costly. So we decided to create a simple buck converter as part of our bigger project. Mind you, this simulation may be seem to be bleak and simple compared to other devices, but this one is a simple test run for our bigger project which will be out in a couple years. And you are going to be a part of it. Think of it as an alpha software that designers use to experiment on before creating a beta project and releasing to the press.

    The project is funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF) Improving Undergraduate STEM Education program (DUE-1712146) from June 2017 to May 2020.
